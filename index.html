Path to DSBS

Admission & Selection Process

Our multi-stage selection process is designed to evaluate candidate credentials, communication competence, analytical skills, and leadership drive.

01

Online Inquiry / Registration

Fill out the application inquiry form on our landing page. Submit basic academic credentials and pay the application fee.

02

Submit Entrance Scores

Provide a valid scorecard from any national-level management exam: CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, KMAT, GMAT, NMAT, ATMA, SNAP, or take the DSBS Entrance Test.

03

Micro-Presentation & Group Discussion

Shortlisted candidates participate in a micro-presentation and a group discussion (GD) to demonstrate analytical thinking and presentation skills.

04

Personal Interview (PI)

Interact directly with our faculty panel in a personal interview session evaluating career goals, core domain interests, and communication skills.

05

Admission Offer & Enrollment

Successful candidates receive an Admission Offer Letter. Secure your seat by paying the registration fees within the specified timeline.
